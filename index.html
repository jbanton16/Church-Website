Our Faith

Greenhills Word Fellowship is a non-denominational Full Gospel Christian fellowship that believing that there is One God who manifested Himself as the Father, the Word and the Holy Spirit (1 John 5:7) and by His Grace through faith, we are saved (Ephesians 2:5-8) and believes in an end-time Message of Restoration of Faith (Malachi 4:5-6) and the promised body change and Rapture for the believers (1 Cor 15:51-54) (1 Thess 4:15-17)

About Us

Greenhills Word Fellowship is a gathering of families of believers who loves to worship the Lord in Spirit and Truth, and studying the Word of God with joy, helping each other to grow in the Grace of the Lord, not just believing the Gospel but manifesting it in our lives, to show God is alive among His people.

Our Focus

We believe that we are now living in the last days, where the promised coming of the Lord is at hand, just waiting for the completion of His church, the fulness of the Gentiles (Romans 11:25).


And God opened the mystery of the Bible by the Message of the Hour that called His Bride and gave them Rapturing faith.


As part of His Bride, we seek, not just to gather in His Word but to live it by His grace, giving Him honor and praise, getting ready for His coming.

The Message

Not for one moment do i bring a message to the people that they may follow me, or join my church, or start some fellowship and organization. I have never done that and will not do that now. I have no interest in those thingsm but I do have an interest in the things of God and people, and if I can accomplish just one thing I will be satisfied. That one thing is to see established a true spiritual relatioship between God and men, wherein men become new creations in Christ, filled with His Spirit and live according to His Word. I would invite, plead and warn all to hear His voice at this time, and yield your lives completely to Him, even as I trust in my heart that I have given my all to Him. God bless you, and may His coming rejoice your heart.

- Rev. William Marrion Branham
