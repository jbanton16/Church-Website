Greenhills Word Fellowship

The Greenhills Word Fellowship is a non-denominational Full Gospel Christian church that believes in an end-time Message that will restore the “heart” of its believers to “the faith” of the original Apostolic fathers (Mal. 4:5-6) and will prepare them for the body change promised in I Thes. 4:16-17 and I Cor. 15:51-54.

We hope you find encouragement and spiritual guidance thru our website or by joining us in our services. It is our utmost desire to be able to help you start your Christian walk and in some way be a blessing along the way, until at last we see our Savior’s face at that glorious meeting in the air. God richly bless you.
